About us

In a small town on the outskirts of Moscow, there lived a young man named Alexey Maslov. He was a passionate film lover and dreamed of creating his own cinema where the audience could enjoy watching movies in a cozy and comfortable environment.After much thought and discussion with friends and family, Alexey decided to make his dream come true. He started looking for a suitable location for his cinema and soon found an old abandoned cinema on the outskirts of the city.Alexey renovated the building, updated the interior and equipped the cinema with modern technology. He named his new cinema WinVideo in honor of his passion for cinema and love of winning.WinVideo quickly became a popular destination among the residents of the city. Film festivals, film screenings and various events were held here. Alexey Maslov became known as a talented businessman and organizer of cultural events.Over time, WinVideo turned into a real cultural center of the city. Not only film screenings were held here, but also exhibitions, concerts
